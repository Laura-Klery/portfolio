About Us

Welcome to the official site, if you have arrived here it is that you want to see a show of quality without ruining yourself. Our circus is for you! Our to propose magics tricks where reality and imagination become one. Our magicians propose for you a word without limit. Our clowns meanwhile will entertain you whith their jokes and skits to which they will make you participate. We are increasingly confronted with a fact that peaple are afraid of clowns, that why ours don't have a costume but just make-up. Our tamers and our animals will make you shudder; you will see big wild felines to turn into nice little cats; all without whip. As for our comedians what to say... they are wonderful, they do everthing to push their limits and the laws of physics to offer you a grandiose spectacle worthy of the greatest stuntmen of Hollywood, our bikers are not outdone in their big cage you can see them start in two and as and when increase their number up to six!

Our Circus is also renowned for its commitment to animal welfare and the cause of children that's why we are committed to donating a quater of our revenue to child protection associations as well as animal protection assocaitions. We also had a park with several large enclosures so that our animals can let off steam when we are not on tour but don't worry when we are on the road the cages are big enough for them to be at ease.

Finally, if you seek a good dose of adrenaline don't hesitate and comme to see us during our performances.

PS: Be careful! All our tours are done by professionals, don't try to remake them at home, you could hurt yourself.
